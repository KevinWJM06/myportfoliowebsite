Design Projects

UI/UX Design Project

App created for an assignment for a fictional green sustainable fashion company named 'Eco-Threads'.
This company aims to sell only clothing sourced from eco-friendly sources with sustainable pracices.
I designed this website to be easy to navigate and visually appealing with green being the primary colour to further empahize on the 'eco' theme overall.
A mobile app version was also created to further enhance the user experience.

  • Skills: Figma
  • Role: UI/UX Designer
  • Category:Website & Mobile App Design

UI/UX Design Project

UI/UX design for an online course app named 'Learn@RP' that provides courses for students to take and learn at their own pace.
This app contains a home page, courses page and lessons page with hamburger menu to navigate the app.
The colour scheme is primarily a shade of green from the Republic Polytechnic's brand colour to emphasize the theme of the school.
The app also contains a loading spinner feature that appears to simulate loading in an actual app.

  • Skills: Figma
  • Role: UI/UX Designer
  • Category: Mobile App Design

Graphic Design

This is a instagram grid design for a fictional travel company named 'Travelly'.
